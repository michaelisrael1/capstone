\Hypertarget{md_README_autotoc_md0}%
 MAAP is a web-\/based application that centralizes record-\/keeping and administrative workflows for organizations supporting individuals with special needs including students and adults. The portal stores client profiles, support plans, medical and educational histories, contact details, staff assignments, case notes, and activity logs to help teams coordinate care and maintain accurate, accessible records.

Key features\+:


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Centralized client and staff profiles with demographic, contact, and support data.
\item Case notes, activity logs, and support-\/plan tracking to document progress.
\item Search, filtering, and basic reporting tools to help staff find and summarize data.
\item Import/export and conversion utilities for migrating existing records (see {\ttfamily conversion/}).
\item A simple, responsive frontend for dashboards and profile management (see {\ttfamily Madonna/}).
\item A page dedicated to a messaging board, fitted with filtering options and media upload.
